Rules of Typing Trainer

1. Typing Accuracy

Typing Trainer challenges you to type the displayed text as accurately as possible. Each keystroke matters — accuracy is calculated based on correct characters typed relative to the total number of characters.

  • Typing the wrong letter counts as a mistake.
  • Skipping letters or adding extra characters reduces your accuracy.
  • Corrections (such as using backspace) are allowed but recorded for statistics.

2. Word Completion

Words must be completed exactly as shown. Pressing space submits the current word. Mistakes are recorded if a word is typed incorrectly.

3. Timing Rules

Depending on the selected mode:

  • Time Mode: Type as many words as possible within the time limit (15, 30, 60, 120 seconds).
  • Words Mode: Complete a specific number of words (10, 25, 50, 100) as fast as possible.
  • Quote Mode: Type an entire quote correctly without skipping characters.

4. Mistakes and Corrections

Mistakes are normal. You can use backspace to correct typos, but excessive corrections may lower your final accuracy.

5. Fair Play

Manual typing only — bots, macros, or automated scripts are prohibited. Focus on genuine practice to improve your skills honestly.

6. Personalization and Settings

Customize your experience by selecting different modes, adjusting text types, and switching themes. Settings are saved locally on your device only.

7. Updates and Changes

Typing Trainer may evolve over time with new features, improvements, and optimizations. Keep practicing and check for updates regularly!
