Security Policy

Data Protection

Typing Trainer operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any data externally. This local-only processing model ensures that your data remains private and secure.

Third-Party Services

Currently, Typing Trainer does not integrate any third-party services that could affect user security, such as analytics providers, cloud storage, or authentication systems.

Vulnerabilities

Since Typing Trainer runs client-side only, vulnerabilities are minimal. We regularly monitor for potential threats and ensure all public assets are served over secure HTTPS connections.

Reporting Issues

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in Typing Trainer, please contact us immediately at amtubedg@gmail.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will work to address issues as soon as possible.
