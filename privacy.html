Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26.04.2025

1. Information We Collect

Typing Trainer does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). All interactions, including typing activity, are processed entirely on your device. No typing data, personal usage patterns, or device identifiers are recorded or sent to external servers.

Your engagement with Typing Trainer remains private and local to your browser session.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

Typing Trainer does not utilize traditional cookies or external tracking scripts to monitor users. However, certain browser-based technologies such as local storage may be optionally used in the future to save user settings (e.g., selected theme, language preference).

These settings remain on your device and are never transmitted externally.

If third-party ads are introduced (e.g., Google AdSense), external services may store their own cookies or tracking mechanisms, governed by their own privacy policies. Typing Trainer has no direct control over third-party cookies.

3. Third-Party Services

At this time, Typing Trainer operates without any integration with external platforms such as:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics),
  • Authentication systems (e.g., OAuth),
  • Content delivery services.

If the application incorporates third-party services in the future, clear and visible notices will be provided to users along with options to manage or opt out where feasible.

5. Data Retention

Since Typing Trainer does not collect or store any personal data, there is no data retention policy required. Any user-generated settings stored via local storage on your device can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Typing Trainer is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from users under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, no special child-specific privacy protections are required.
